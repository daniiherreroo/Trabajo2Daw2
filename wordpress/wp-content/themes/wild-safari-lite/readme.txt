== Description ==
Wild Safari Lite is a elegant and refined, minimal and sophisticated, stylish and gracious, feature-rich and intuitively navigable, gorgeous and seamless, modern, polished, and professionally designed animal and pets WordPress theme.This theme can also be used for business, corporate, restaurant, hotel, construction and multipurpose website. Its a very unique and purposeful theme, dedicated to pet care centers, pet stores, animal lovers, dog lovers blog and all pet related business.
